ABOUT I2COMSAPP 2026

I2COMSAPP is a premier international conference bringing together researchers, 
academics, and industry professionals to discuss advances in artificial 
intelligence and its practical applications in developing countries.

📌 CONFERENCE TOPICS:
• Machine Learning & Deep Learning
• Natural Language Processing
• Computer Vision
• Robotics & Automation
• AI Ethics & Governance
• Big Data Analytics
• AI in Healthcare
• AI in Education
• Smart Cities & IoT
• Digital Transformation

SUBMISSION GUIDELINES:

📝 Format:
   - PDF document format only
   - 8-15 pages including references
   - Double column layout
   - Springer LNNS formatting

📎 Font & Style:
   - Font: Times New Roman, 10pt
   - Line spacing: Single
   - Margins: 2.5cm on all sides

👥 Authorship:
   - Maximum 5 authors per paper
   - Provide email for all co-authors
   - Include author affiliations

📧 Language:
   - Primary: English
   - Abstracts accepted in French or Arabic

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

SUBMISSION PROCESS:

1. Download the paper template (Word or LaTeX)
2. Prepare your paper following the guidelines
3. Convert to PDF format
4. Submit through the conference portal at: i2comsapp2026.org
5. Include PDF file of your complete paper

All papers will be peer-reviewed by qualified reviewers from the Scientific Committee.
